Academics Philosophy Our Program Learning Goals Learning Goals the ability to analyze evidence and evaluate arguments for cogency the ability to construct rationally persuasive arguments for and against various philosophical positions and theoretical perspectives to imaginatively engage texts and to creatively construct interpretations to approach the study of philosophy with intellectual humility and patience and accept and engage complexity to be willing to explore all views with care and patience so as to avoid oversimplification to identify and demonstrate familiarity with major Western philosophers and philosophical movements to comprehend the distinctive contribution of Asian philosophies and demonstrate understanding of one or more of those philosophies to understand how
