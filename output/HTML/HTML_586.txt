<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><a id="What We Offer"></a><strong>What we offer</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The instructional technology and media services (ITMS) team assists faculty, students, and staff with their technology interests and needs at Muhlenberg.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We provide open hours</span>&nbsp;<span style="font-weight: 400;">for faculty and staff questions (refer below for our calendar!), and support the learning management system (Canvas), video (Panopto), collaboration tools (such as VoiceThread, Hypothes.is), web conferencing (Zoom), among other resources.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Do know that you may place Canvas-related requests using our </span><a href="https://www.jotform.com/82323974965974" target="_blank"><strong>Canvas support request form!</strong></a></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Also, know that we have developed an </span><a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1v5wvCOi7z9dWJpb2zx9Rhp9z7L9gbOL6uuxBNuwSPGc/edit?usp=sharing">ITMS worksheet for resources and frequently asked questions related to the new Canvas gradebook.</a></strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;"><a id="Accessibility/Documentation"></a><strong>Accessibility/Documentation</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Throughout our web pages, you will encounter links to our documentation, provided via Google Docs.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To download any of these items, you will navigate to âfile,â then select âdownload as.â This step allows you access to our documents both online and offline, as well as in your preferred file format (.doc, .pdf, among others). You may find that this format also helps with translation of text to preferred language.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p>We have included clickable table of contents to navigate to particular sections of a document, although if you select the paper icon tab in the left corner of the current document (next to the page ruler and directly below the printer icon), Google automatically provides an outline view of the text provided.&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We strive to provide accessible, universally designed content, inclusive of our documentation. Please contact us at </span><strong><a href="mailto:itms@muhlenberg.edu">itms@muhlenberg.edu</a></strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> with any questions or with suggestions for future documentation.</span></p>
