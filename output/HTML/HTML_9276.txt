      <h2>Class Movie Screenings:</h2>
<h3>B-02</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Professors schedule movie screenings in Trexler Library outside of standard class time; these can be movies owned by the library or a personal copy. These screenings are typically held in B-02, the video classroom, which is located along the west wall of the B-Level concourse. If a faculty member wishes to schedule a viewing, please contact the&nbsp;Library's Administrative Assistant&nbsp;(</span><a href="mailto:lindsayliiro@muhlenberg.edu">lindsayliiro@muhlenberg.edu</a><span style="font-weight: 400;">).</span></p>
<h2>Individual Movie Screenings:</h2>
<h3>A/V Alcove Viewing Stations</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our collection includes films on DVD, Blu-ray, and VHS that are available for check out. Viewing stations for each media type are available along the south wall of the A-Level concourse.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<h3>Library Desktop Computers</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">All desktop computers in the building have Corel WinDVD to play the DVDs in our collection.</span></p>
<h3>Seminar A</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Seminar A is a viewing room equipped with a wall TV unit with a built-in DVD player as well as an HDMI port to connect to a computer. It also contains a wall-mounted whiteboard. This room is available on a first-come, first-served basis. Seminar A can be found on the west side of the A-Level concourse in the elevator alcove.</span></p>
<h3>External Optical Drives</h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Lending Services Desk now has external DVD/Blu-Ray drives that are available for checkout. These drives connect to PCs/Macs' USB ports and work with little to no installation required. Unfortunately, these drives are not compatible with Chromebooks.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: right;"><em>Reviewed 2021</em></p>
