    <section class="col2-wide">
      <!--<h2>Room Scheduling & Usage</h2>-->
      <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Various offices on campus control the scheduling of rooms in the library building as follows:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Fulford Room, B-01 Library Instruction Room, Rare Books Room, B-02 Classroom, Seminar Rooms, and the Information Commons: </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Contact Trexler Library at 484-664-3551.</span></li>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Only Faculty and Staff can reserve the Fulford Room, B-01 Library Instruction Room, Rare Books Room, B-02 Classroom, Seminar A, and the Information Commons. Students, Faculty, and Staff can reserve Seminar C.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">Note:</span></em><span style="font-weight: 400;"> The Library maintains first priority in scheduling the B-01 Library Instruction Room and the Information Commons in all cases. The Library may bump scheduled users from these rooms up to 72 hours before a scheduled event. </span><strong><em>For the first two months of each semester, the Information Commons and B-01 Classroom are unavailable for outside reservations due to the heavy library instruction use.</em></strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Otherwise, the B01 Library Instruction Room and Information Commons are available for open access when classes are not scheduled. Usage and scheduling exceptions will be granted at the discretion of the Library Director.</span></li>
</ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Curriculum Lab: </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Contact Registrar's Office at 484-664-3190 or Education Department at 484-664-3298.&nbsp;</span></li>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Curriculum Lab is scheduled by Registrar's Office for classes during the day. The Lab remains accessible to education students when classes are not scheduled.</span></li>
</ul>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Group study rooms are also available throughout the library. In all cases, groups will be given priority usage over individuals in these group study rooms. Individual users may be asked to move to a different space when a group needs the room for study activities.</span></p>
<h3><strong>Need to use the One Button Studio?</strong></h3>
<p><strong><a href="http://muhlenberg.libcal.com/booking/one-button-studio" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Schedule a time</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"><span>&nbsp;</span>to use the One Button Studio.</span></strong></p>
