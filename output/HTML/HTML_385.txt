  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Political science is a dynamic field of study that explores dimensions of power and the ways in which governmental systems shape and reflect ideas, norms, institutions, cultures and political behavior.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A commitment to engaged democratic citizenshipâlocally, nationally, globallyâis core to the mission of our program. A majority of students engage in long or short-term study abroad experiences and/or a wide variety of internshipsâvolunteering for political campaigns, interning in legal offices and courts of law or working for nonprofit organizations on issues such as immigration, reproductive rights and educational equality.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The program equips students with research concepts, theories and methods and tangible leadership qualities required for effective engagement in a globalized, interdependent world. All students undertake independent research in close mentoring relationships with program faculty.</span></p>
