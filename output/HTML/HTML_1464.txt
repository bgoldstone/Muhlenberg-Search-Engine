  <h3 style="text-align: left;"><span><a id="community-access"></a>Community Access</span></h3>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">ELL Conversation Groups (South Mountain&nbsp;Middle School)</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;">Students facilitate and create groups to reinforce conversational English skills that adults and teens in local communities would like to enhance. 1-1 opportunities available as well.&nbsp;</p>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400; max-width: 250;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.15.29 PM-1-225x218.png" alt="photo of Berg Votes logo" style="     " /></span></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Berg Votes</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Register students to vote, engage in the electoral process, host issue-based forums and more!</span>&nbsp;</p>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400; max-width: 250;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.16.22 PM-225x225.png" alt="Image of Best Buddies logo" style="     " /></span></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Best Buddies</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Best Buddies is an international organization that pairs people with and without intellectual disabilities in one-on-one friendships. The colleges program pairs college students in friendships with people in the local community who are referred by the ARC. Our campus chapter is large (50 members) and involves the most students of any campus in the Lehigh Valley. The chapter hosts around 4 events per semester for the buddy pairs, and the pairs are expected to call one another every week and see each other 2 times a month.</span></p>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400; max-width: 250;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.27.14 PM-235x167.png" alt="Image of a bike and the name Community Bike Works" style="     " /></span></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Community Bike Works</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400; width: 200;">Muhlenberg students have volunteered both through the drop-in after-school program time at Bike Works and as volunteers through the Earn-A-Bike program. Contrary to what the name might imply, you don‚t need to know about bicycles to volunteer at Bike Works!</span>&nbsp;</p>
<hr>
<h3><span><a id="education-access"></a>Education Access</span></h3>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.09.35 PM-225x220.png" alt="photo of two volunteers holding the American Reads sign board" style="     " /></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">America Reads (Roosevelt Elementary School)</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;">As part of a national program aimed at improving reading amongst kindergarten through 3rd grade, Muhlenberg students collaborate with teachers to reinforce literacy lessons. Small groups and one-on-one time allow for Muhlenberg students to integrate into the classroom throughout the course of the semester in this two-hour/week minimum program. Virtual opportunities for this program are forthcoming!&nbsp;</p>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.11.09 PM-225x220.png" alt="Image of Casa Guadalupe drawing" style="     " /></span></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Casa Guadalupe</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Casa Guadalupe is a community center in the 1st Ward of Allentown that has worked with primarily Latinx residents since 1969. Students work with the Pa‚Lante Education program, connecting with young people through the arts, coding, physical activity and general after-school activities.&nbsp;</span></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">College Connect (suspended)</h5>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">3rd grade (Muhlenberg Elementary, Hayes Elementary, Jefferson Elementary, Roosevelt Elementary) and 8th (South Mountain Middle School) visit the College to learn more about College and think about the future.</span></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Jefferson Visits (suspended)</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Students from each grade level visit the College for different, focused, lessons. Second grade does a literacy visit, third grade does College Connect, fourth grade does a science visit and fifth grade does a circus visit.&nbsp;</span></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">JCC of the Lehigh Valley- Early Childhood Education</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Work with teachers and kids from pre-toddler (approx one year) to pre-K.</span></strong></p>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.11.45 PM-225x214.png" alt="photo of the men of color network volunteers in front of Jefferson Elementary School" style="     " /></span></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Bow Tie Club (men of color preferred)</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;">Pairs Muhlenberg students who identify as men with&nbsp;kids at South Mountain Middle School who identify as boys in a mentoring program intended to build relationships to support success.</p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Super Girls</h5>
<p>Engage with 4th and 5th grade girls with an focus on self-esteem, girl power and representation.</p>
<hr>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span><a id="arts-access"></a>Arts Access</span></h3>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.12.31 PM-225x167.png" alt="Photo of volunteers at Art Day Gallery Day" style="     " /></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Art Day at Hayes Elementary School</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;">Art Day takes place every week at Hayes Elementary School. Muhlenberg volunteers work with elementary students to create a variety of different art pieces focusing on different techniques and artists from around the globe each visit. From pointillism to Van Gogh, the kids and students love working together to create masterpieces.&nbsp;</p>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.13.11 PM-225x216.png" alt="Photo of Jefferson Theatre Arts volunteers " style="     " /></span></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Hays Elementary Theatre Arts</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Berg students engage elementary school kids in theatre games and techniques through this after-school program.</span></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</h5>
<p><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.16.48 PM-225x228.png" alt="photo of Magical Memories volunteers in Disney Princess costumes" style="     " /></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Magical Memories</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Volunteers dress as various characters and visit local schools and health care settings to engage young people in a little moment of magic - characters range from super heroes to movie characters and imaginary beings.</span></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Movers &amp; Shakers</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">An after-school program at Roosevelt Elementary that focuses on different skills everyone can apply to help access movement in their bodies. Students have the opportunity throughout, and at the end of the program, to share their creations!&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<hr>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span><a id="healthy-communities"></a>Healthy Communities</span>&nbsp;</h3>
<div style="text-align: justify;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.14.40 PM-225x163.png" alt="photo of volunteers and grandparents involved in Adopt-a-Grandparent " style="     " /></div>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Adopt-a-Grandparent</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;">Muhlenberg students are paired with senior citizens who reside at Phoebe Home, just down the road from the College. They connect in reciprocal relationships intended to support one another, and the whole group comes together several times per semester for fun, social activities.</p>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.13.21 PM-225x166.png" alt="Photo of Daybreak volunteers digging at the garden" style="     " /></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Daybreak</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Daybreak is a drop-in program for adults who are in treatment for mental health concerns, experiencing homelessness or have HIV/AIDS. Students form supportive relationships with Daybreak members and are able to learn from one another.</span>&nbsp;</p>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.13.36 PM-225x145.png" alt="Photo of a volunteer with elementary students at Shape It Up!" style="     " /></span></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Shape it Up!</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;">Elementary students connect with kids through this program focused on the importance of physical fitness and nutrition. Collaborative games encourage teamwork and support amongst the kids and Muhlenberg students.</p>
<h4 style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.13.54 PM-225x126.png" alt="Photo of volunteers with The Sanctuary dogs" style="     " /></span></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">The Sanctuary</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Student volunteers at The Sanctuary connect with cats and dogs who are looking for new homes. Cleaning, walking animals and playing create a beneficial experience for all.</span></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Manito Horse Farm&nbsp;</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Support both the traditional and therapeutic riding school by assisting with cleaning and animal care.</span></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Social Butterflies</h5>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Connect with kids in Allentown to support social and emotional learning!</span></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Movers @ Ripple</h5>
<p>Chair movement with adult community members @ Ripple</p>
<hr>
<h3 style="text-align: left;"><span><a id="housing-food-access"></a>Housing &amp; Food Access</span></h3>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Food<strong> Recovery</strong> Network</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"></span>Students partner with Muhlenberg Dining, Daybreak and Turning Point of the Lehigh Valley to reduce catering food waste at the college and support individuals and families at Turning Point and Daybreak.</p>
<p><img src="/media/contentassets/images/campuslife/community-service/Screen Shot 2020-07-21 at 10.16.37 PM-225x164.png" alt="photo of Habitat for Humanity volunteers and participants" style="     " /></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Habitat for Humanity</h5>
<p style="text-align: left;">Seeks to eliminate homelessness and substandard housing through affordable homeownership. Students volunteer weekly at the Re-Store, host events on campus for families, fundraise and more through the campus chapter of this organization.</p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Habitat<strong> for Humanity Re-Store&nbsp;</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Assist with various tasks at this re-use store benefiting Habitat for Humanity.</span></p>
<h5 style="text-align: left;">Jefferson<strong> Elementary Food Pantry</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Muhlenberg students assist with food sorting and distribution at the pantry housed within the school.&nbsp;</span></p>
