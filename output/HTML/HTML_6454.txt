<h2>Muhlenberg Recognizes Scholastic Achievement, Community Impact at 2019 Honors Convocation</h2>



The College community gathered to honor students, faculty and staff with Muhlenberg awards and to celebrate those who received national awards.

<p style="color:grey;font-size:small;">
By:

<a href='<a href="/offices/communications/meetthestaff/billkeller/">Bill Keller</a>'>

  Bill Keller  


</a> 

<span style="text-size:small;">	&nbsp;Tuesday, May 7, 2019  01:49 PM</span></p>

<!-- FULL WIDTH Image -->
<img src="https://www.muhlenberg.edu//media/contentassets/images/newscenter/academics/2019HonorsConvocation.jpg" style="width:100%;" alt="News Image"><br/><span style="color:grey;font-size:small;font-style:italic;">At the May 5 ceremony, James Peck, professor of theatre & dance, delivered the convocation address: "6:55: The Time of Rehearsal in Art and Life."</span><br/><br/>


<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Muhlenberg College Honors Convocation is a formal academic ceremony designed to honor and celebrate leadership and scholarly excellence and achievement. This year marks the College's 171st year of higher education, and perhaps more than at any other time, this event serves to recognize members of the community for their contributions to Muhlenberg College, to their peers and to their disciplines.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Faculty and staff awards included <a href="/news/2019/barrigoldrecognizedat2019honorsconvocation.html">The Class of 1932 Research Professorship</a>, <a href="/news/2019/byunchulcharecognizedat2019honorsconvocation.html">The Crossette Family Faculty Fellowship for International Research, The Donald B. Hoffman Research Fellowship</a>, <a href="/news/2019/emilyorzechrecognizedat2019honorsconvocation.html">The Robert C. Williams Faculty Award</a>, <a href="/news/2019/thomascartellirecognizedat2019honorsconvocation.html">The Daniel J. and Carol Shiner Wilson Grant for the Completion of Scholarly Projects</a>, <a href="/news/2019/kericolabroyrecognizedat2019honorsconvocation.html">The Spira Award</a>, <a href="/news/2019/matthewmoorerecognizedat2019honorsconvocation.html">The Award for the Outstanding Advisor to First-Year Students</a>,&nbsp;<a href="/news/2019/robertmaharrecognizedat2019honorsconvocation.html">The Chairman‚s Award</a> and <a href="/news/2019/brynnmariedorseyandmikebeckerrecognizedat2019honorsconvocation.html">The Hamre Prize</a>.<br><br></span></p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">National Awards</span></h2>
<p><strong>The J. William Fulbright Program<br></strong>Awarded through national competition for graduate study or teaching abroad.</p>
<p>Brigid Deegan '19 (Finalist)<br>Amanda Harper Joseph '19 (Finalist)<br>Morgan Michelson-Kelly '19 (Finalist)<br>Brooke Torjman '19 (Finalist)</p>
<p><strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></strong></p>
<p><strong>NCAA Postgraduate Scholarship<br></strong>Awarded to student-athletes who have an overall grade point average of 3.2 or higher, have performed with distinction as members of a varsity team, are outstanding citizens and excellent role models for the institution and intercollegiate athletics as a whole and intend to continue as a full or part-time graduate student.</p>
<p>Kelly Tornetta '19</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>The Harry S. Truman Scholarship<br></strong>Awarded through national competition for college juniors with outstanding leadership potential who plan to pursue a graduate education and attain a career in public service.</p>
<p>Valerie Weisler '20 (Recipient)<br>Riley Minkoff '20 (Finalist)</p>
<h2><span style="font-weight: 400;">The President‚s Award</span></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This award honors the student organization that has contributed in the most positive way to Muhlenberg College. This year‚s recipient was</span><strong> ‚Berg Votes</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">, a nonpartisan student group that held issues forums, meet the candidate events and voter registration drives in the 2018 election cycle.<br><br></span></p>
