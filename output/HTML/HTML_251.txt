<h3>About the Internship Program</h3>
<p>The Accounting, Business, Economics, and Finance program at Muhlenberg has a flourishing internship program. Students may select an internship through our <a href="https://muhlenberg.joinhandshake.com/login" target="blank">internship database</a> or they can develop an internship through their own contacts. Our internship coordinator, <a href="mailto:michaellondon@muhlenberg.edu" target="blank">Michael London</a>, helps students find, design, and get placed in an internship. Internships may count for course credit but they do not have to, and many students take on internships that do not count for college credit but give them needed experience. For more information please visit our <a href="https://sites.google.com/muhlenberg.edu/muhlenberg-college-academic-in/students?authuser=0" target="blank">Internship Website</a>.<br><br> In any given academic year 50 or more students are typically working in internships in which they are also earning college credit toward graduation. In addition, close to 50 students participate in internships each year that are not for academic credit. Nearly 80% of ABEF students participate in an internship during the pursuit of their major, and over 60% of those have more than one internship while at Muhlenberg.</p>
<p><strong>Internships for credit</strong><br><span>Students can elect to receive course credit for an internship.&nbsp; This option allows you the time to focus on your internship and to be part of a cohort, learning from peers having similar experiences.&nbsp; It is also important that you bring together theory and practice to deepen your learning.&nbsp; Employers will hope to hear you speak about your internship with both scholarship and real world experience.&nbsp; Interactions with faculty and peers bring together knowledge gained through past and present course work, applying it in real time to deal with real work issues.</span><br><br><span>Students&nbsp;have internships at a variety of organizations including</span>:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.airproducts.com/" target="blank">Air Products and Chemicals</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.pplweb.com/" target="blank">PP&amp;L Corporation</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.blackrock.com/" target="blank">BlackRock</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.deloitte.com/view/en_GX/global/index.htm" target="blank">Deloitte &amp; Touche</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.pwc.com/us/en/index.jhtml" target="blank">Pricewaterhouse Coopers</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.athletesperformance.com/" target="blank">Athletes' Performance</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.washingtonwizards.com" target="blank">Monumental Sports &amp; Entertainment</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.reebok.com/us" target="blank">Reebok International</a></li>
<li>And many others</li>
</ul>
<p><br> Students that want to learn more about internship opportunities or about the internship program in the ABEF Department should contact&nbsp;<a href="https://www.muhlenberg.edu/facultysearch/facultyresults/michaellondon/">Michael London</a>&nbsp;(ABEF Internship Director).</p>
<hr>
<p>There are two forms that need to be filled out in order to receive academic credit for your internship experience.</p>
<p>1. <a href="https://form.jotform.com/203148295604152" target="_blank">Internship Registration Form</a><br>Complete this form, including a signature from your site-supervisor, academic advisor, faculty sponsor (it is always <span>Michael London</span>, Internship Director), and yourself. This completed form will be routed to the Office of the Registrar.<br><br> 2. <a href="/academics/abe/internships/internshipdatabaseinformation/">Internship Database Information</a><br>Please complete this online form. It will automatically be sent to&nbsp;Michael London&nbsp;(ABEF Internship Director) and to Kim Clark (ABEF Administrative Assistant).</p></div>

  <h3>Read about their internship experiences:</h3>
<p style="text-align: left; padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Robert Borkow</strong><img src="/media/contentassets/images/academics/abe/R Borkow-230x272.jpg" alt="" style="  padding : 10px;   float : left;" /></p>
<p style="text-align: left; padding-left: 30px;">Majors: Business Administration<br>Class of 2018</p>
<p style="text-align: left; padding-left: 30px;">Northwestern Mutual â New York City, NY</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">I worked as a financial representative intern where I was tasked with client acquisition and some servicing of current clients. Through the experience, I discovered how much I enjoyed working with clients and I found a true passion for wealth management. I also found financial planning very interesting; it was like putting a jigsaw puzzle together. I was the number one intern in the division based on points (policies sold and corresponding premium commission) out of 22. I received a job offer for when I graduate. Grateful to have found this opportunity through the Career Center!&nbsp;</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/robert-borkow-149b11aa">https://www.linkedin.com/in/robert-borkow-149b11aa</a></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">&nbsp;</p>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Elizabeth Clark</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/academics/abe/E Clark-230x153.jpg" alt="" style="  padding : 10px;   float : left;" />Majors: Finance &amp; Accounting<br>Deloitte â&nbsp; Parsippany, NJ</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">I worked with the Deloitte Audit Innovation team and on Honeywell as a client. Most of my time was spent working on projects that were related to Honeywell's quarter two statements. These included controls testing, roll forwards, leadsheets, and ROMMS. My team was very helpful and supportive, which made the internship so rewarding. I was happy that so many of the finance courses use Excel because a lot of my time was spent working in Excel. I felt well prepared.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/elizabeth-clark/">https://www.linkedin.com/in/elizabeth-clark/</a></p>
<hr>
<p style="padding-left: 30px; text-align: left;"><strong><img src="/media/contentassets/images/academics/abe/C Gary-230x345.jpg" alt="" style="  padding : 10px;   float : left;" />Chelsea Gary</strong></p>
