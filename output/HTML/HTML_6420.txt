<td valign="top">
<p style="margin-top: 4px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-size: 80%;"><strong><a id="dearborn"></a>Karen Dearborn, Dance Program Chair<br></strong></p>
<p style="margin-top: 4px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-size: 80%;">Professor, Ballet, Composition, and Dance History;<br>&nbsp; &nbsp;Co-Director of Dance Recruitment;<br>&nbsp; &nbsp;Muhlenberg Community Dance Center (MCDC) Executive Director<span>&nbsp;<br></span>M.F.A., Connecticut College; B.S., Radford University</p>
<p style="font-size: 70%;">Karen&nbsp;Dearborn is the founder and Director of Dance for Muhlenberg College. She has choreographed more than 80 works in concert dance, theatre, and musical theatre, including National Tours of the Tony Award-winning National Theatre of the Deaf and Equity theatre companies. As a dancer, she performed professionally for 12 years in concert dance and musical theatre productions. Her research interests include performance studies, disability studies, and dance pedagogy, and her writing has been published in the<span>&nbsp;</span><em>Journal of Dance Education</em><span>&nbsp;</span>and the book<span>&nbsp;</span><em>Performing Magic on the Western Stage</em>. She has presented papers at numerous conferences including National Dance Educators Organization (NDEO), Congress on Research in Dance, and the National Dance Association. Currently she is Treasurer of the American College Dance Association (ACDA), a member of CORPS de Ballet International, NDEO, Society of Dance History Scholars and AEA. Distinguished teaching awards include the Limback and the Spira.</p>
<p style="font-size: 80%; text-align: right;">she/her/hers<strong>&nbsp; |&nbsp;&nbsp;<a href="mailto:dearborn@muhlenberg.edu">dearborn@muhlenberg.edu</a>&nbsp; |&nbsp; x3335</strong></p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td align="center" valign="top" width="120"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/theatreampdance/images/dancefaculty/cruz-austin-w.jpg" alt="cruz-austin" style="     " /></td>
<td valign="top">
<p style="margin-top: 4px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-size: 80%;"><strong><a id="cruzaustin"></a>Heidi Cruz-Austin,&nbsp;</strong><strong>Director of Dance Recruitment</strong></p>
<p style="margin-top: 4px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-size: 80%;">Visiting Assistant Professor: Ballet and Dance Education;<br>&nbsp; &nbsp;Co-Director of Dance Recruitment<br>M.F.A., University of the Arts, Philadelphia</p>
