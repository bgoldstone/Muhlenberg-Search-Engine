<p>Through <strong>world-class studio training</strong> and <strong>rigorous academics</strong>, the Theatre &amp; Dance Department aims to produce <strong>flexible, collaborative, inspired artist-thinkers</strong>, well-equipped to contribute to the greater good.</p>
<p>The Muhlenberg Theatre &amp; Dance experience offers a <strong>direct learning partnership</strong> among faculty and studentsâin the classroom, the studio and the performance laboratory. Students are <strong>nurtured to succeed</strong> and <strong>encouraged to take risks</strong>. The department offers students daily opportunities to foster the most important goal of the liberal arts experience: education<strong> of the whole person</strong>.</p>
<p>Theatre &amp; Dance faculty and staff have developed a multi-faceted approach to education that combines <strong>exciting creative experience</strong> with <strong>rigorous professional standards</strong>. Students are challenged as artists in the studio and the classroom, working closely with <strong>distinguished faculty</strong> and <strong>internationally recognized guest artists</strong> to achieve a conceptual understanding and a practical working knowledge of the performing arts.</p></div>
