<h2>Appstream Virtual Lab</h2>
  <p>Appstream Virtual Lab is the College's virtual software solution.&nbsp;The Appstream Virtual Lab provides students, faculty and staff remote access to campus standard software normally available in physical labs on campus. Since its launch in August 2020, over twenty-five applications have been made available to use.&nbsp;Note: Google Drive IS the storage solution for Appstream. You MUST connect to Google Drive to successfully access, save and store files. Watch the video below to learn how!</p>

<li><strong>I connected my Google Drive storage, but I can't access the files in an application</strong>?&nbsp;<br>Try signing out and sign back in to the session to check if your Google Drive storage is available. You will need to wait a few minutes while the expired session closes. If you are still unable to access your Google Drive files, you will want to&nbsp;<a href="https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/3466521?hl=en" target="_blank">remove Appstream's access in your Google Account</a> and then login to Appstream to setup your storage again.&nbsp;</li>
<li><strong>My session is taking a long time to load</strong><br>Appstream Virtual lab is&nbsp;has a set startup time of 2 minutes and 30 seconds for each session. You will see a countdown clock while the session is being prepared showing REAL TIME exactly how much time is left until the session is available to use. If you recently closed a session, you will need to wait a few minutes before starting a new one.&nbsp;</li>
<li><strong>I uploaded files to Appstream during my last session and they are no longer there</strong><br>Appstream's storage solution is Google Drive. While you may upload files for temporary use during a session, they will not be available after that session ends.&nbsp;</li>
<li><strong>My session timed out</strong><br>Appstream sessions will timeout if you are idle for 60 minutes. This was previously set to 15 minutes idle and has been increased.</li>
