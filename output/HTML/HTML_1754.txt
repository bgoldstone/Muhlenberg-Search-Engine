<h3 align="justify">Disabilities Resources for Adult Learning Professionals</h3>
<div class="content-add05"><a href="#national">National Resources</a><br> <a href="#ldo">Learning Disabilities Organizations</a><br> <a href="#Psychiatric">Psychiatric &amp; Medical Disabilities Organizations</a><br> <a href="#ADD">Attention Deficit Disorder Organizations</a><br> <a href="#Vision">Vision Impaired Organizations</a><br> <a href="#Speech">Speech &amp; Hearing Impaired Organizations</a><br><hr>
<h3 style="text-align: left;" align="center"><a id="national"></a>National Resources<br><br></h3>
<p><strong>Americans with Disabilities Act<br></strong>ADA Information Line<br>1-800-514-0301 (voice)<br>1-800-514-0383 (TDD)<br><strong><a href="http://www.ADA.gov">www.ADA.gov</a></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The Americans with Disabilities Act, enacted July 26, 1990 prohibits discrimination and ensures equal opportunity for persons with disabilities in employment, State and local government services, public accommodations, commercial facilities, and transportation.</p>
<p><strong>Association on Higher Education and Disability (AHEAD)<br></strong>107 Commerce Center Drive, Suite 204<br>Huntersville, NC&nbsp; 28078<br>(704) 947-7779 (Voice/TDD)<br>(704) 948-7779 (fax)&nbsp; <br><strong><a href="http://www.ahead.org">http://www.ahead.org</a></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">AHEAD is the premiere professional association committed to full participation of persons with disabilities in postsecondary education. AHEAD promotes leadership and exemplary practices; provides professional development, disseminates information; and orchestrates resources through partnership and collaboration</p>
<p><strong>The Job Accommodations Network (JAN)</strong><strong><br> </strong>PO Box 6080<br> Morgantown, WV 26506-6080<br> (800) 526-7234 Â· v/TTY in the US or (800) ADA-WORK Â· v/TTY in the US<br>Voice 800-526-7234<br>TTY 8-781-9403<br>(304) 293-5407 Â· fax<br> <strong><a href="http://janweb.icdi.wvu.edu/">http://janweb.icdi.wvu.edu/</a></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">JAN represents the most comprehensive resource for job accommodations available.&nbsp; JAN provides information on job accommodations and information on the Americans with Disability Act.&nbsp; JANâs work helps employers, people with disabilities, rehabilitation professionals, and people affected by disability.</p>
<p><strong>American Association of People with Disabilities (AAPD)</strong><strong><br> </strong>Main Office<br> 2013 H Street, NW, 5<sup>th</sup> Floor<br> Washington, DC 20006<br> (800) 840-8844&nbsp;&nbsp; fax 866-536-4461<br> (202) 457-0046 Â· v/TTY<br> <strong><a href="http://www.aapd.com">http://www.aapd.com</a></strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">AAPD is the largest, national, nonprofit cross-disability member organization in the US, dedicated to ensuring economic self-sufficiency &amp; political empowerment for the 56 million Americans with disabilities. AAPD works in coalition with other disability organizations for the full implementation &amp; enforcement of disability nondiscrimination laws, particularly the ADA of 1990 and the Rehabilitation Act of 1973.</p>
<p><strong>The National Rehabilitation Information Center (NARIC)</strong><strong><br> </strong>8400 Corporate Drive, Suite 500<br> Landover, MD 20785<br> (800) 346-2742&nbsp;<br> (301) 459-43263 fax<br> (301) 459-5984 Â· TTY<br> <strong><a href="http://www.naric.com/">http://www.naric.com</a></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">NARIC is an on-line, information Center funded by the National Institute on Disability and Rehabilitation Research to serve members, health professionals, educators, rehab counselors, students, librarians, administrators, researchers, and other professionals.</p>
<p><strong>The National Dissemination Center for Children with Disabilities (NICHCY)</strong><strong><br> </strong>P.O. Box 1492<br> Washington,&nbsp;DC&nbsp;20013-1492<br> (800) 695-0285 Â· v/TTY<br> (202) 884-8441&nbsp;Â·&nbsp;fax<br> <strong><a href="http://www.nichcy.org/">http://www.nichcy.org</a></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">NICHCY, a national information &amp; referral center, provides information on disabilities/disability-related issues for families, educators, and professionals.&nbsp; NICHCY provides information and makes referrals in areas related to: specific disabilities, early intervention, special disability organizations, professional associations, educations rights, transitions to adult life, etc.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Effective 9/30/14 NIHCY no longer in existence. Redirected to <a href="http://www.parentcenterhub.org">www.parentcenterhub.org</a></p>
