<h2>Capstone Projects</h2>
  <h3>&nbsp;</h3>
<p>Perhaps the best demonstration of The School of Continuing Studiesâ cutting-edge approach to learning is the Accelerated Degree program, where teams of students learn collaboratively, bringing a blend of professional and experiential skills to the classroom.</p>
<p align="left">Final or âcapstoneâ projects are required of all groups for graduation and illustrate each cohortâs newly acquired proficiencies. The capstones address a need or problem in the surrounding community.</p>
<p>The following list represents recent capstone projects and demonstrates how School of Continuing Studies students are giving back to the community in which they live and work.</p></div>
