The readings are password-protected, to be used only by the Muhlenberg community. To get the password, email Troy Dwyer, Faculty Director of the <em>Sedehi Diversity Project</em>, at <a href="mailto:dwyer@muhlenberg.edu">dwyer@muhlenberg.edu</a>.</p>
