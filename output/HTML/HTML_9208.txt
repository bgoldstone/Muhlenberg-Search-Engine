               <h2>Digital Collections</h2>
               <p class="page-txt">Trexler Library has been digitizing its unique holdings for several years, in an effort to broaden the reach of these collections for scholarship in the classroom and beyond. It is continually a work in progress, but we are pleased to provide access to these special materials.</p>
               <h3>Muhlenberg College History</h3>
               <ul class="no-style">
                   <li class="no-style"><a href="https://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/muhlenbergweekly" target="_blank">The Muhlenberg Weekly</a></li>
                   <li><a href="http://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/ciarla" target="_blank">The College yearbook, the Ciarla</a></li>
                   <li><a href="https://library.artstor.org/#/collection/87732075" target="_blank">Muhlenberg College Postcard Collection</a></li>
                   <li><a href="https://trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/photoarchive/" target="_blank">Muhlenberg College Historical Photo Archive</a></li>
                   <li><a href="https://library.artstor.org/#/collection/100005761" target="_blank">Muhlenberg College World War II Correspondence Collection</a></li>
<li><a href="https://trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/mc_oralhistory/" target="_blank">Muhlenberg College Oral History Collections</a></li>
               </ul>
			   
			   <h3>Lehigh Valley LGBT Community Archive</h3>
						<ul class="no-style">
							<li><a href="https://trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/lgbt_oralhistory/" target="_blank">Lehigh Valley LGBT Oral History Repository</a></li>
							<li><a href="https://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/LGBT_digital" target="_blank">Digitized magazines, newsletters, meeting minutes, and more from the Archive</a></li>
						</ul>
			   
               <h3>Other Special Collections</h3>
             <ul class="no-style">
                   <li><a href="https://trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/textiles/" target="_blank">The History that Speaks for Itself: Oral Histories from the Textile and Needle Trade Industry of the Lehigh Valley (2022)</a></li>
                   <li><a href="https://library.artstor.org/#/collection/87731862" target="_blank">The Ray R. Brennen Map Collection</a></li>
                   <li><a href="https://library.artstor.org/#/collection/87730556" target="_blank">The Robert C. Horn Papyri Collection</a></li>
                   <li><a href="https://library.artstor.org/#/collection/87732194" target="_blank">The Helen Richards Horn Bookplate Collection</a></li>
                   <li><a href="https://library.artstor.org/#/collection/87731239" target="_blank">The Muhlenberg Family Papers (1779-1854)</a></li>
