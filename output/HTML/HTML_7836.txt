<h2>Muhlenberg Students, Faculty, Staff Honored at 2020 Honors Convocation</h2>



The virtual ceremony celebrated academic achievement, community engagement, original research and commitment to the College.

<p style="color:#4D4D4D;font-size:small;">
By:



  Bill Keller  



<span style="text-size:small;">	&nbsp;Monday, May 4, 2020  10:15 AM</span></p>

<!-- FULL WIDTH Image -->



<p>The Muhlenberg College Honors Convocation is a formal academic ceremony designed to honor and celebrate leadership and scholarly excellence and achievement. This year marks the College's 172nd year of higher education and, due to the nationwide impact of COVID-19, the first time the ceremony was conducted virtually.</p>
<p>Faculty and staff awards included <a href="/news/2020/grantscottawardedtheclassof1932researchprofessorship.html">The Class of 1932 Research Professorship</a>, <a href="/news/2020/robles-morenoawardedthecrossettefellowship.html">The Crossette Family Faculty Fellowship for International Research</a>, <a href="/news/2020/eduardoolidguerreroawardedthedonaldbhoffmanresearchfellowship.html">The Donald B. Hoffman Research Fellowship</a>, <a href="/news/2020/maurafinkelsteinawardedtherobertcwilliamsfacultyaward.html">The Robert C. Williams Faculty Award</a>, <a href="/news/2020/2020danieljandcarolshinerwilsongrants.html">The Daniel J. and Carol Shiner Wilson Grant for the Completion of Scholarly Projects</a>, <a href="/news/2020/lindseynagyawardedspiraprize.html">The Spira Award</a>, <a href="/news/2020/caseymillerrecipientof2020outstandingadvisoraward.html">The Award for the Outstanding Advisor to First-Year Students</a>, The Student Government Award, <a href="/news/2020/muhlenbergannounces2020facultyrisingscholarawardrecipients.html">The Faculty Rising Scholars Award</a>,&nbsp;<a href="/news/2020/donnakish-goodlingnamedrecipientofchairmansaward.html">The Chairman‚s Award</a> and <a href="/news/2020/muhlenbergnamesrecipientsof2020hamreprize.html">The Hamre Prize</a>.</p>
<p>The faculty address,&nbsp;‚See You in the Classroom,‚ was given by Professor of <a href="/academics/polisci/">Political Science</a> <a href="/facultysearch/facultyresults/lanetheamathews-schultz/">Lanethea Mathews-Schultz</a>.</p>
<p align="center"><iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ZIWD4IDDWD4" width="850" height="478"></iframe></p>
<h3>National Awards</h3>
<p><strong>The J. William Fulbright Program</strong></p>
<p>Awarded through a national competition for graduate study or teaching abroad.</p>
<p><strong>Ryan M. Dimmick ‚20</strong><br><strong>Olivia M. Toner ‚20</strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>The President‚s Award</h3>
<p>This award honors the student organization that has contributed in the most positive way to Muhlenberg College. This year‚s recipient was <strong>Top Naach Dance Club</strong>, the College's Bollywood dance group. Receiving the award on behalf of Top Naach was <strong>Nisha Godbole '20</strong>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Dean of Students Awards</h3>
<p><strong>The Student Government Award<br> </strong>Awarded to a current member of the faculty, staff or management team for outstanding service to students, over a period of at least three years, in promoting student involvement, responsibility and action.<br> <strong>Pamela Moschini, Director, Office of Disability Services</strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p style='font-size:small;font-style:italic;'><strong>About Muhlenberg College </strong><br/>Founded in 1848, Muhlenberg is a highly selective, private, four-year residential, liberal arts college offering baccalaureate and graduate programs. With an enrollment of approximately 2,200 students, Muhlenberg College is dedicated to shaping creative, compassionate, collaborative leaders through rigorous academic programs in the arts, humanities, natural sciences and social sciences; selected preprofessional programs, including accounting, business, education and public health; and progressive workforce-focused post-baccalaureate certificates and master‚s degrees. Located in Allentown, Pennsylvania, approximately 90 miles west of New York City, Muhlenberg is a member of the Centennial Conference, competing in 22 varsity sports. Muhlenberg is affiliated with the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America.</p>
