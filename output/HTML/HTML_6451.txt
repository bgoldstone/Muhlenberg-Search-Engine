<p style="font-size: 90%;"><strong>The Guest Artist program offers students</strong><span>&nbsp;</span>in both theatre and dance the opportunity to&nbsp;work and study&nbsp;with&nbsp;world-class artists-in-residence. The Department engages numerous guest artists each year as teachers, actors, directors, composers, choreographers, and designers.</p>
<p style="font-size: 90%;">Past residencies have included:</p>
<ul style="font-size: 90%;">
<li>Tony Award-nominated makeup artist <strong>Joe Dulude II</strong></li>
<li>Modern dance company<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>Urban Bush Women</strong></li>
<li><strong>Peter Schumann</strong><span>&nbsp;</span>and the<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>Bread and Puppet Theatre</strong></li>
<li><strong>The Chuck Davis African American Dance Ensemble</strong></li>
<li>Composer<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>Charles Strouse</strong>, playwright<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>Mark St. Germain</strong>, and director/choreographer<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>Barbara Siman</strong></li>
<li><strong>Figurentheater: Wilde and Vogel</strong>, internationally-acclaimed puppet theatre artists</li>
<li><strong>Rosalba Rol√≥n</strong><span>&nbsp;</span><strong>and Pregones Theater</strong></li>
<li>Obie Award-winning poet and playwright<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>Mac Wellman</strong></li>
<li>Nationally respected choreographer<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>Danny Buraczeski</strong><span>&nbsp;</span>and his company Jazz Dance</li>
<li>Internationally acclaimed modern choreographers<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>David Dorfman</strong><span>&nbsp;</span>and<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>Doug Varone</strong></li>
<li>Vocalist<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>Marni Nixon</strong></li>
</ul>
<p><strong><span>This year, we're compiling a roster of world-class Resident Guest Artists to conduct virtual workshops and performances throughout the academic year. These residencies will include public events available to the entire Muhlenberg and Lehigh Valley communities, as well as masterclasses for Theatre &amp; Dance students. Stay tuned for details!</span></strong></p></div>

<p style="font-size: 80%; margin-top: 4px; margin-bottom: 0px;"><strong>Amelia Bande</strong></p>
<p style="font-size: 70%; margin-top: 4px; margin-bottom: 0px;">Amelia Bande<span>&nbsp;(she/her) is a writer, performer, music maker and language teacher originally from Chile. She uses text and visuals to create live capsules of intimacy and low-fi musicals. Her solo and collaborative work has been shown at Artists Space, The Poetry Project, Storm King Arts Center, Participant Inc., BOFFO Performance Festival, EFA Project Space and more. She has been an artist in residence at WORM Filmwerkplaats, The Shandaken Project, Yaddo, FIAR, Human Resources LA, and Tinkledy Springs.
