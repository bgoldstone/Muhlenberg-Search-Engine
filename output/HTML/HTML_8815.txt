<h2>Community Engagement&nbsp;</h2>
<p><span>If you would like to contact/ get information about any of these organizations, please email&nbsp;</span><a href="mailto:studentactivities@muhlenberg.edu">studentactivities@muhlenberg.edu</a></p>
<hr>
<p><strong>Adopt-A-Grandparent</strong></p>
<p><span>Members of Adopt-A-Grandparent visit the elderly at the Phoebe home, providing friendship and company through arts and crafts, and conversation. By offering their time, students are able to interact with the Allentown Community. There is a blossoming bond between Muhlenberg students and the Phoebe home. Students have the opportunity to be paired with residents by keeping in contact through messages, phone calls, and visits.</span></p>
<h6><strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></strong></h6>
<p><a href="https://aphioberg.wixsite.com/apo-berg"><strong>Alpha Phi Omega</strong></a></p>
<p><span>APO is a co-ed service fraternity that strives to promote service on campus. Our main focus is to do service not only on Muhlenberg's campus, but also the surrounding neighborhood of Allentown</span></p>
<p><span>Follow us!</span></p>
<p><span>Facebook:</span><a href="https://www.facebook.com/Alpha-Phi-Omega-Xi-Alpha-Chapter-at-Muhlenberg-College-1611191109110276/"><span>Alpha Phi Omega xi Chapter @ Muhlenberg</span></a></p>
<p><span>Instagram:<span>&nbsp;</span></span><span><a href="https://www.instagram.com/muhlenbergapo/">@muhlenbergapo</a></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Animal Shelter &amp; Rescue Club</strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Best Buddies</strong></p>
<p><span>Best Buddies is a club on campus that promotes inclusiveness. It joins together community members with intellectual and physical disabilities and creates lifelong friends with college students at Muhlenberg.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="https://www.muhlenbergconnect.com/cardinalkey"><strong>Cardinal Key Society</strong></a></p>
<p><span>The Society traces its roots to May 28, 1940 when six members of the Class of 1942 founded the group to serve Muhlenberg College and extend goodwill to visitors. The Society has grown over the years to provide a cohesive program for student involvement in the greater College community, specifically with alumni. Members, known as Keys, also work to develop traditions and instill a sense of school spirit among Muhlenberg students. In partnership with the Office of Alumni Affairs and the Career Center, Keysâ collaborate on events such as Alumni Weekend, Commencement, Honors Convocation, Candlelight Carols, and other campus initiatives. Students are eligible to apply for admission to the Cardinal Key Society during the fall of their freshman and sophomore years. If selected, students join the Society the following spring and serve through January of their senior year.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Colleges Against Cancer: Relay For Life</strong></p>
<p><span>Colleges Against Cancer also known as Relay For Life is an organization to raise money for cancer research.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span>Follow us!</span></p>
<p><span>Facebook:<span>&nbsp;</span></span><a href="https://www.facebook.com/Relay-For-Life-of-Muhlenberg-College-160365874050451/"><span>Relay-For-Life-of-Muhlenberg-College</span></a></p>
<p><span>Instagram:<span>&nbsp;</span></span><span><a href="https://instagram.com/muhlenbergcac?utm_source=ig_profile_share&amp;igshid=1evuwquige1fb">@muhlenbergcac</a></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="https://www.bergems.org/"><strong>EMS @ Muhlenberg College&nbsp;</strong></a></p>
<p><span>Muhlenberg College Emergency Medical Services (MCEMS) is a student-run, volunteer EMS organization that provides medical care to care to students, faculty, and guests on campus 24/7 throughout the academic year.</span></p>
<p><span>Follow Us!</span></p>
<p><span>Facebook:<span>&nbsp;</span></span><a href="https://www.facebook.com/MuhlenbergEMS/"><span>MuhlenbergEMS</span></a></p>
<p><span>Instagram:<span>&nbsp;</span></span><span><a href="https://instagram.com/berg_ems?igshid=1hp0e4oompsof">@Berg_ems</a></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Habitat for Humanity</strong></p>
<p><span>Habitat for Humanity at Muhlenberg College works with the local affiliate of the organization to address the issue of housing insecurity in the Lehigh Valley. The club volunteers weekly at the Re-Store, participates in builds within local communities, assists with fundraising activities and plans/organizes Family Fun Nights. Come join us!</span></p>
<p><span>Follow us!</span></p>
<p><span>Facebook:<span>&nbsp;</span></span><span><a href="https://www.facebook.com/Muhlenberg-College-Habitat-for-Humanity-154540967930931/">Habitat for Humanity @ Muhlenberg College&nbsp;</a></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Magical Memories: Muhlenberg's Character Visitation Club</strong></p>
<p><span>Magical Memories is Muhlenberg's Character Visitation Club. We travel to elementary schools, hospitals and other local organizations dressed as characters (Princesses, Superheroes, etc.) to spread positivity. We are advised by Muhlenberg's Office of Community Engagement.</span></p>
<p><span>Follow us!</span></p>
<p><span>Facebook:<span>&nbsp;</span></span><a href="https://www.facebook.com/magicalmemoriesmuhlenberg"><span>MagicalMemoriesMuhlenberg</span></a></p>
<p><span>Instagram:</span><span><a href="https://www.instagram.com/magicalmemoriesmuhlenberg/">@magicalmemoriesmuhlenberg</a></span></p>
