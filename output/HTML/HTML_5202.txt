<td><span style="font-weight: 400;">For more than 100 years, the </span><strong>Muhlenberg College School of Continuing Studies </strong>has understood the needs of adult learners. Muhlenberg College professors and advisors work hand-in-hand with our students to personalize their educational journey. These students include:
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Adults who want to start or finish a bachelor's degree</strong></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong><a href="https://www.muhlenberg.edu/continuingstudies/prospectivestudents/veteransportal/">Veterans or active military personnel</a></strong></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Adults with a bachelor's degree who are seeking a second degree or certificate in a specific content area</strong></li>
</ul>
Weâre excited to get to know you, to learn about your passions, and begin to explore how a Muhlenberg experience can help transform your life, build your community or enhance your organization.
