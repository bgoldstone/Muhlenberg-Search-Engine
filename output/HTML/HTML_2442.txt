<h3>Required Clearances</h3>
<p>All student teachers and fieldwork students must provide the public school building administrators copies of the following:</p>
<p><strong><a href="#Act114">Federal Criminal History - Act 114 (fingerprinting)</a><br> <a href="#Act34">Pennsylvania State Criminal History Background Check: PATCH - Act 34</a><br> <a href="#Act151">Child Abuse History Clearance - Act 151</a><br> <a href="#TBTest">Tuberculin Test</a></strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><a id="Act114"></a>Federal Criminal History</strong></p>
<p>Effective April 1, 2007, all student teaching candidates and fieldwork students must have an FBI fingerprint clearance completed before entering the public schools.</p>
<p><strong>Please read the following directions carefully</strong><strong>.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Fee: $22.60</strong>.<span>&nbsp; </span>All applicants will now receive an unofficial copy of their report in the mail.</p>
<p><strong>If you can provide your own transportation to the fingerprinting site, use the following instructions:</strong></p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Registration - The applicant must register prior to going to the fingerprint site.</strong><span> </span>Walk-in service is allowed but all applicants are required to complete pre-enrollment in the new Universal Enrollment system.<span>&nbsp;</span>Pre-enrollment can be completed online or over the phone.<span>&nbsp; </span>The registration website is available online 24 hours/day, seven day per week at <a href="https://uenroll.identogo.com/">https://uenroll.identogo.com</a>.
<p>When registering online, an applicant must use the appropriate agency specific Service Code to ensure they are processed for the correct agency and/or applicant type.<span>&nbsp; </span>Use the Service Code <strong>1KG6RT.</strong><strong><span>&nbsp; </span></strong></p>
<p>Telephonic registration is available at 1-844-321-2101 Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. EST.<span>&nbsp; </span>During the pre-enrollment process all demographic data for the applicant is collected along with notices about identification requirements and other important information.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li><strong>On-line Registration Process - </strong>After logging into the site and entering the Service Code provided above, complete the registration process.<span> </span><strong>Select Schedule Appointment.<span>&nbsp; </span></strong>Proceed with the on-line registration appointment scheduling process as instructed on the website.<br><br></li>
<li><strong>Fingerprint Locations - After registration, the applicant proceeds to the fingerprint site for payment and fingerprinting.</strong><span> </span>The locations of the fingerprint sites and hours of operation are posted on the IDEMIAâs website at the above link.<span>&nbsp; </span>The Education Department will provide directions and hours for the site closest to Muhlenberg College.<br><br>The location of fingerprint sites may change over time; applicants are encouraged to confirm the site location nearest to their location.</li>
<li><strong>Fingerprinting - </strong>At the fingerprint site the Enrollment Agents (EA) manages the fingerprint collection process.<span> </span>The fingerprint transaction begins when the EA reviews the applicantâs qualified State or Federal photo ID before processing the applicantâs transaction.<span>&nbsp; </span>A list of approved ID types is attached.<br><br>Applicants will not be processed if they cannot produce an acceptable photo ID.<span>&nbsp; </span>After the identity of the applicant has been established, all ten fingers are scanned to complete the process.<span>&nbsp; </span>The entire fingerprint capture process should take no more than three to five minutes.</li>
</ol>
<p><em>The Education Department is able to look up your fingerprint results after you have visited the fingerprinting site.&nbsp; Please bring the receipt you received after your fingerprinting was completed listing your <strong>UEID</strong> number to Cecile in Moyer 327 during regular office hours.</em></p>
<p><strong>If you will be using the Education Department Fingerprint Shuttle as transportation to the fingerprinting site</strong>, please attend the scheduling meeting on the announced date and time for registration and appointment scheduling.<span>&nbsp; </span>Please do not schedule an appointment outside this meeting.<span>&nbsp; </span>If you do so we cannot assure your appointment time will be compatible with our shuttle time.</p>
<p><em>The Education Department is able to look up your fingerprint results after you have visited the fingerprinting site.&nbsp; Please bring the receipt you received after your fingerprinting was completed listing your <strong>UEID</strong> number to Cecile in Moyer 327 during regular office hours.</em></p>
<p>Fingerprint Services Customer Service Call Center - 1-844-321-2101</p>
</div>
<div class="content-add10">
<p><strong><a id="Act34"></a>Act 34 Criminal Record Check</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Go to the Pennsylvania Access To Criminal History website and apply for your criminal record check online with the Pennsylvania State Police at:&nbsp;<a href="https://epatch.state.pa.us/Home.jsp">https://epatch.state.pa.us/Home.jsp<br><br></a><a href="https://epatch.state.pa.us/Home.jsp" target="_blank"></a></li>
<li>On the PA Home page select <strong>Submit a New Record Check</strong> (gray box)</li>
<li>On this page, please review the <strong>Terms and Conditions</strong> for the use of PATCH.<span>&nbsp; </span>Scroll to the bottom of the page.<span>&nbsp; </span>Here, by clicking the <strong>Accept</strong> button you will move into the page to begin the process.<span>&nbsp; </span>If you click Decline, you cannot go further and get your clearances online.<br><br></li>
<li>Select <strong>Individual Request</strong> and click <strong>CONTINUE<br><br></strong></li>
<li>On the <strong>Requestor Information</strong> page beside <strong>Reason for Request</strong>, click on the down arrow, then click on <strong>Other.</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>From this point on you will need to move through the remaining pages as directed to pay for and receive your clearance printout.<span>&nbsp; </span>You will receive the clearance printout after the application has been completed online unless there is a need for extensive research by the PSP.<span>&nbsp; </span>A debit/credit card is required for payment for each clearance request and the cost is currently $22.00.<span>&nbsp; </span>Please do not forget to print out your clearance.</p>
<p><strong>Please be sure to print out the actual certification form before exiting the website.<span>&nbsp; </span>(See attached example of what your certification form should look like.)</strong></p>
<p>If you have questions about the Pennsylvania State Police Request for Criminal Record Checks form (SP4 164), please call: (717) 783-9973 or toll free 1-888-783-7972.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
</div>
<div class="content-add10">
<p><strong><a id="Act151"></a>Child Abuse History Clearance</strong></p>
<p><strong>Effective July 1, 2018:</strong></p>
<p>The Pennsylvania Child Abuse History Clearance application can be submitted online.<span>&nbsp; </span>Once you enter the link below you will be directed to the Child Welfare Portal where you must create an account or log in if you already have an account.<span>&nbsp; </span>Note: you will need an email address to create an account.</p>
<p><strong>ELECTRONIC SUBMISSION</strong></p>
<p>Creating an account and submitting your clearance application online will give you immediate access to your results or the status of your results if your results cannot be processed immediately.</p>
