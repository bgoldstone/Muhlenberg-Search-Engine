<h2>Managing your Financial Aid Online Portal</h2>
  <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Your </span><a href="https://netp.muhlenberg.edu/NetPartnerStudent/Logon.aspx?ReturnUrl=%2fNetPartnerStudent%2f"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Financial Aid Portal</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> is activated once you apply to Muhlenberg College and it will allow you to :</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Check the status of your financial aid application by monitoring the document page</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Allow access to the Muhlenberg College Scholarship and Aid Application that you must complete</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">And, when accepted view your financial aid award and accept, decline, or reduce any portion of the award </span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The best internet browsers for using your Financial Aid Online Portal are </span></li>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Internet Explorer 11</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Firefox (Most recent version)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Google Chrome (Most recent version</span></li>
</ul>
</ul>
<p><a href="https://netp.muhlenberg.edu/NetPartnerStudent/Logon.aspx?ReturnUrl=%2fNetPartnerStudent%2f">Access your portal here.</a></p>
<p><strong>Prospective Students and Current Students needing to -Activate your Financial Aid Online Account</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Your user ID :</p>
<p><strong>Applicants&nbsp;for Admission:<br></strong>Your Student ID is the e-mail address you indicated for yourself on the Common App Admission Application.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Current Muhlenberg College students:<br></strong>Your Student ID is your Muhlenberg College e-mail</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on First Time User</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You will then be asked to set up your security questions and answers and it is case sensitive (Remember these because the Office of Financial Aid Staff cannot see the questions and answers you set up)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Create your password - Your Password must be at least 9 characters in length. &nbsp;It must contain at least 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, 1 number and 1 special character (!@#$%^&amp;*). &nbsp;Your password will expire every 90 days and you must have 5 unique passwords before you can recycle one. (The Office of Financial Aid staff cannot see your password so make a password you can remember)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Accept the Terms and Conditions of the site.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You will then be returned to the main login page. &nbsp;Login with your e-mail address and your newly created Password and hit SUBMIT.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the upper left had side of the page you should see MENU. &nbsp;You can click here to pick the page you would like to go to.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></strong></p>
<p><strong>Returning Users</strong></p>
<ol>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Remember the Student ID for&nbsp;<strong>Applicants&nbsp;for Admission</strong></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">&nbsp;is the studentâs e-mail address that was provided on&nbsp; the Common App Admission Application.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span><strong>Current Students</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">&nbsp;will use their Muhlenberg College e-mail.</span></p>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Type in your password and Click on SUBMIT.</span></p>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;">
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you have forgotten your password, click on Forgot Your Password. &nbsp;You will be prompted to enter your Student ID e-mail address and answer your security questions.</span></p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">After 6 unsuccessful attempts to log in your account will be locked. &nbsp;Please contact the Office of Financial Aid at 484-664-3175 or </span><a href="mailto:finaid@muhlenberg.edu"><span style="font-weight: 400;">finaid@muhlenberg.edu</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and someone will be happy to unlock it for you. &nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">See&nbsp;<a href="/media/contentassets/pdf/about/finaid/Code IA.pdf">code IA</a>&nbsp;for assistance with the most common mistakes.</span></p>
<p><strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As always, if you have any questions or concerns or need any help with the application process, please let us know!&nbsp;</span>We may be reached at 484-664-3175 or&nbsp;<a href="mailto:finaid@muhlenberg.edu">finaid@muhlenberg.edu</a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p></div>
