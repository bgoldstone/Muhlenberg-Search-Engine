<h2>Ciarla FAQ</h2>
  <p><strong><strong>Q: How does my graduate get a free 2022 yearbook?<br></strong></strong>A:&nbsp;Every senior who provides a&nbsp;<a href="/student-life/ciarla/seniorphotos/">senior portrait</a>&nbsp;for the 2022&nbsp;<em>Ciarla</em>&nbsp;Yearbook&nbsp;will receive&nbsp;<em>one complimentary copy</em>&nbsp;of the yearbook. They can also choose to order&nbsp;an&nbsp;electronic yearbook&nbsp;to accompany their print yearbook for only $10.&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li>Seniors do not need to order a yearbook if they have provided a portrait.&nbsp;<span>The 2022&nbsp;<em>Ciarla</em>&nbsp;will be distributed&nbsp;in the fall and mailed directly to the graduate&nbsp;at the&nbsp;home address on file on June 1 in the Registrar's office. See the&nbsp;<a href="/student-life/ciarla/seniorphotos/">Senior Photos</a>&nbsp;page for portrait information.</span><em><strong><br><br></strong></em></li>
<li>While the yearbook staff strongly encourages students to utilize&nbsp;Lauren Studios for their senior portrait, students who are unable to do so may submit a high-resolution photo in front of a neutral-colored backdrop. An image taken by a professional photographer is preferred.</li>
<li>
<p>Any senior submitting a photo not taken by Lauren Studios must&nbsp;send the portrait&nbsp;to&nbsp;<a href="mailto:ciarla@muhlenberg.edu" target="_blank">ciarla@muhlenberg.edu</a>&nbsp;by <em>May&nbsp;1</em> and request their name and address be placed on the mailing list for their complimentary copy of the 2022 <em>Ciarla.</em></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Q: My student is studying abroad. Can I have his/her photo taken elsewhere and send it to Muhlenberg?</strong><br>A: If&nbsp;your student is&nbsp;unable to use Lauren Studios on campus, they may submit a portrait by email to&nbsp;<a href="mailto:ciarla@muhlenberg.edu">ciarla@muhlenberg.edu<span>&nbsp;</span></a>and&nbsp;request their portrait be included in the 2022<span>&nbsp;</span><em>Ciarla</em><span>&nbsp;</span>so they can receive their complimentary copy.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Q: Why isnât my studentâs headshot in the yearbook?</strong><br>A: The most common reason is that your student likely did not attend any of the photo sessions on campus or submit an alternate photo.</p>
<p><strong>Q: I didnât know my senior received a complimentary 2022 yearbook and I ordered one. Can I return it for a refund?</strong><br>A:&nbsp;<span>Cancelations must be requested&nbsp;prior to the&nbsp;</span><span>June 1</span><span><span>&nbsp;</span>deadline.</span> According to Balfour's policy, a fee may be applied to all canceled orders.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Q: How do I order&nbsp;a Senior Pride ad?</strong><br>A: These ads are a great way to congratulate seniors and wish them luck in their future endeavors. Graduates are also welcome to submit an ad to memorialize their time at Muhlenberg.</p>
<p>It's easy to create a personalized ad using the Balfour Online Store.&nbsp;Visit&nbsp;<a href="https://www.balfour.com/pennsylvania/allentown/muhlenberg-college/yearbook-packages" target="_blank">Muhlenberg College <em>Ciarla</em> Yearbook Packages/Balfour Online Store.</a>&nbsp;Within 24 hours you will receive instructions via email to go Balfour.com &gt; Log in &gt; My Orders to begin uploading your photos and message. When complete, your ad will be delivered to the yearbook staff.&nbsp;<span>See our&nbsp;</span><a href="/student-life/ciarla/parentprideads/">Senior Pride Ads</a><span><span>&nbsp;</span>page&nbsp;to learn about ad&nbsp;sizes,&nbsp;prices and&nbsp;more.</span></p>
<p><span><strong>Q: What size Senior Pride Ad do most people&nbsp;order?</strong><br><span>A: The most popular sizes tend to be quarter-page or half-page ads; however we offer a variety of options for different budgets. See our <a href="/student-life/ciarla/parentprideads/">Senior Pride Ads</a></span><span><span>&nbsp;</span>page for more details.</span></span></p>
<p><strong>Q: I missed the deadline for ordering a Senior Pride ad. Will you still accept my order?</strong><br>A:&nbsp;Senior&nbsp;Pride Ad&nbsp;submissions for the 2022&nbsp;<em>Ciarla</em>&nbsp;have been extended to May 27. Publication of any ad received after the final deadline cannot be guaranteed.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Q: If we do not like the headshot taken in the fall of my studentâs senior year, can&nbsp;they have the photo retaken when Lauren Studios is on campus in the spring?</strong><br>A: Yes. Spring dates will be announced on the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.muhlenberg.edu/student-life/ciarla/seniorphotos/" target="_blank">Senior Photos</a> page.</p>
<p><strong>Q: My student graduated last year and did not have&nbsp;their headshot taken for the yearbook. Can&nbsp;they have a photo taken and placed in this yearâs book so we have yearbook proof they went to Muhlenberg?</strong><br>A: No. The yearbook focuses on the current senior class and undergraduates.</p>
