      <h2>Contactless Checkout Service</h2>
      <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">For enhanced safety and convenience, Trexler Library has implemented a Contactless Checkout and Pick-up service for our students, faculty, and staff members to obtain physical library materials.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Our online catalog, </span><a href="https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Encompass Search</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> enables patrons to </span><a href="https://youtu.be/R4lItpgdPxc?t=48" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">virtually browse our shelves</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and explore what we have to offer. This reduces contact with library materials and makes it easy for you to place a hold on items you would like to check out. Placing a hold through Encompass Search must be done before items can be checked out. Please view the </span><a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1UjlsHbRuooh3nwroH5zapIbyH1abAX9H/view?usp=sharing" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">PDF instructions</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> or </span><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R4lItpgdPxc" target="_blank"><span style="font-weight: 400;">video overview</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> for a detailed look at this quick and easy process. Once the hold has been placed, a Library staff member will retrieve your item for you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">After your item has been prepared and checked out to you, you will receive an email, sent to your Muhlenberg email address, explaining three options for receiving your item.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">1. Come to the Library with your Muhlenberg ID and pick up the material yourself from the Muhlenberg Room on Level A. Please view the </span><a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/about/hours/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">building hours</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and schedule your arrival accordingly.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">NOTE:</span></span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> If you live locally but do not have permission to access campus facilities, the email will provide you with an alternative method for picking up your library materials.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">2. Respond to the email with a preferred shipping address if you are currently residing off-campus and would like your material mailed to you</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">NOTE:</span></span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> We will not be mailing material to those who live locally. If you live locally but do not have permission to access campus facilities, the email will provide you with an alternative method for picking up your library material.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">3. Request a scan of selected pages, sections, and/or chapters of the item to be sent to your Muhlenberg email address.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">NOTE:</span></span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> In accordance with the Fair Use doctrine, only 20% of a work can be scanned and emailed to you.</span></p>
<p><strong>You will have 5 days to either pick up your material or respond to the email before it will be checked back in and reshelved.</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">With the contactless checkout service in place, Trexler Library will not be conducting in-person checkouts. </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">To ensure equity, any item that is brought to the Lending Service desk for checkout will need to be reviewed to confirm that no prior holds have been placed by other patrons via Encompass Search</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">. If there are no holds on the item, the item will be checked out to you by the next business day and you will receive an email indicating the item is ready for you. If there are prior holds on the item, an Interlibrary Loan request will be placed for you to expedite your ability to get the item you need.</span></strong></p>
<p>If you require any further assistance, please contact Lending Services (<a href="mailto:circulationdesk@muhlenberg.edu">circulationdesk@muhlenberg.edu</a>).</p>
