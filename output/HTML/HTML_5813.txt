<p>&zwnj;&zwnj;&zwnj;&zwnj;<br /><img src="/media/contentassets/images/academics/biology/faculty/Dan Klem-100x150.jpg" alt="" style="  padding : 10px;   float : left;" /><strong>Daniel Klem Jr.</strong></p>
<p>Professor<br /> Sarkis Acopian Professor of Ornithology and Conservation Biology<br /> Shankweiler 443S<br />484-664-3259<br /> <a href="mailto:klem@muhlenberg.edu">klem@muhlenberg.edu</a></p>
<p align="center" class="style3">Link to the: <a href="/academics/biology/acopiancenterforornithology/">Acopian Center for Ornithology</a></p>
<p><strong>Education<br /><br /></strong> B. A. Biology, Wilkes College<br /> M. A. Biology, Hofstra University<br /> Ph. D. Zoology, Southern Illinois University at Carbondale<br /> D.Sc. Wilkes University</p>
<p><strong>Courses Taught<br /><br /></strong> Concepts of Biology: Biology of Birds (BIO 104)<br /> Principles of Biology II (BIO 151)<br /> Comparative Anatomy (BIO 245)<br /> Comparative Embryology (BIO 240)<br /> Ethology (BIO 265)<br /> Ornithology (BIO 255)<br /> Biological Statistics (BIO 380)</p>
<p><strong>Research Interests<br /><br /></strong> Animal behavior, conservation, ecology, evolution, ornithology, and the natural history of animals; special attention given to vertebrates, statistics, and wildlife mortality resulting from the actions of man.</p>
<p><strong>Professional Society Membership</strong></p>
<p>Animal Behavior Society; American Birding Association; American Ornithologists&rsquo; Union; Association of Field Ornithologists; Birdlife International; Colonial Waterbirds Society; Cape May Bird Observatory; Cooper Ornithological Society; Eastern Bird-Banding Association; Ecological Society of America; Hawk Mountain Sanctuary Association; Hawk Migration Association of North America; National Audubon Society; Northeastern Bird-Banding Association; Ornithological Council (Board of Trustees, 2006 &ndash; present), Pennsylvania Academy of Science; Pennsylvania Biological Survey; Pennsylvania Society of Ornithology; National Institute for Urban Wildlife; Raptor Research Foundation; Sigma Xi; Society for Conservation Biology; The Institute for Bird Populations; The Wetlands Institute; The Wildlife Society; Wilson Ornithological Society (Council Member, 2005 &ndash;2009).</p>
<p><strong>Honors</strong></p>
<p>Graduate School: Teaching Assistantships (Hofstra University), Teaching Assistantships (Southern Illinois</p>
<p>University at Carbondale), Special Doctoral Fellowship (Southern Illinois University at Carbondale), Research Fellowship (Southern Illinois University at Carbondale); Elective Member of American Ornithologists&rsquo; Union Darbaker Prize (Award for Best Paper Using Microscopic Techniques) from Pennsylvania Academy of Science; Lindback Foundation Award for Teaching Excellence from Muhlenberg College; Hawk Migration Association of North America (HMANA) Service Award; Hoffman Research Fellowship (1993-94); Doctor of Science (D.Sc.,Wilkes University 2000); Warden's Award from Hawk Mountain Sanctuary Association (2000); Detroit Audubon Society Conservation Leadership Award (2007); Pennsylvania Ornithological Society Conservation Award (2008); Research Grants from: American University of Armenia (Affiliate of University of California),</p>
<p>CPFilms, Inc., Huge&rsquo; Company, Inc., Hawk Mountain Sanctuary Association, Muhlenberg College, Pittsburgh Plate</p>
<p>Glass Foundation, The Wetlands Institute, and U. S. Fish and Wildlife Service.</p>
<p><strong>Select Publications and Papers Presented at Professional Meetings</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Oral and written presentations on avian predatory behavior, birds of prey migration, comparative anatomy and histology of the avian alimentary tract, seabird distribution over near shore waters of the western Atlantic, and man-caused avian mortality in general and the consequences of window collisions in particular.</p>
<p>Oral Presentations at Annual Meetings: Animal Behavior Society, American Ornithologists' Union, American Bird Conservancy, Birds and Buildings Conference, Joint Cooper and Wilson Ornithological Societies, International Wildlife Rehabilitation Council, Wilson Ornithological Society, National Symposium on Urban Wildlife.</p>
<p>Published Scientific Papers: Animal Behaviour, Journal of Field Ornithology, National Symposium on Urban Wildlife, Proceedings of the Hawk Migration Association of North America, Proceedings of the Pennsylvania Academy of Science, The Auk, The Wilson Journal of Ornithology (formerly: The Wilson Bulletin). Select citations of publications follow.</p>
