  <p>These are some compiled resources for folks in the Lehigh Valley. We recognize this list is not exhaustive, and might fall out of date quickly, so we direct you to visit the United Way of the Greater Lehigh Valley‚s <a href="https://www.unitedwayglv.org/gethelp">COVID-19 resource page</a>, dial 211 or <a href="https://www.uwp.org/programs/2-1-1/">visit 211 online</a> or utilize <a href="https://www.valleyyouthhouse.org/covid-19-resources-for-youth-families/?fbclid=IwAR0TezQ3OVBeXsrf1kL_a0aib6lwQ1et1KuwmvfjoDBxOCrLYF9CRNilIpQ">this website</a> compiled by Valley Youth House for further information. Below are some resources divided by topic area.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Health</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>NHCLV</strong>- Neighborhood Health Clinic of the Lehigh Valley- Federally Qualified Healthcare Center&nbsp;</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Allentown Health Center:<br>(located inside Casa Guadalupe Center)<br><a href="https://www.google.com/maps/dir//218+North+2nd+Street,+Allentown,+PA+18102">218 North 2nd Street, Allentown, PA 18102<br></a>Phone- 610-841-8400<br><a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/allentown-es/">https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/allentown-es/</a><a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/allentown-es/"><br></a><a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/allentown">https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/allentown</a><a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/allentown/">/<br><br></a></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Bethlehem Health Center: <br>Fowler Family Health Center<br>(inside Donegan Elementary School)<br><a href="https://www.google.com/maps/dir//1210+East+4th+Street,+Bethlehem,+PA+18015">1210 East 4th Street, Bethlehem, PA 18015<br></a>En espanol: <a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/bethlehem-es/">https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/bethlehem-es/</a><a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/bethlehem-es/"><br></a>In English: <a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/bethlehem/">https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/bethlehem/</a><a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/bethlehem/"><br><br></a></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Easton Health Center:<br>Two Rivers Health and Wellness Building<br><a href="https://www.google.com/maps/dir//1101+Northampton+Street,+Easton,+PA+18042">1101 Northampton Street, Easton, PA 18042<br></a>PHONE: 484-544-3113<br><a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/easton/">https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/easton/</a><a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/easton/"><br></a><a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/easton-es/">https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/easton-es/</a><a href="https://www.nhclv.org/health-centers/easton-es/"><br></a><strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>WIC </strong>Pennsylvania - Women, Infant &amp; Children‚s Food and Nutrition Program</p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;">610-435-5673 for appointment at Casa Guadalupe WIC Center, 218 N 2nd St., Allentown, PA 18102</p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;">610-691-6491 for appointment at Hispanic Center of the Lehigh Valley, 502 E. 4th St., 2nd FL, Bethlehem, PA 18015-1882</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">WIC Online:<br>En espanol: <a href="https://www.pawic.com/BienvenidosWIC.aspx">https://www.pawic.com/BienvenidosWIC.aspx</a><a href="https://www.pawic.com/BienvenidosWIC.aspx"><br></a>In English: <a href="https://www.pawic.com/default.aspx">https://www.pawic.com/default.aspx</a></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Many grocery stores accept WIC benefits as payment for items intended for your youngest kid at home (WIC supports kids under age 5).<br><br></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Sobriety &amp; Support Meetings </strong>- Some AA locations are closed but others are hosting online <a href="https://www.aalv.org/meetings">https://www.aalv.org/meetings</a>. If you choose to continue meeting with your support group, please practice social distancing.&nbsp;</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://www.na.org/meetingsearch/text-results.php?country=USA&amp;state=Pennsylvania&amp;city=Lehigh%20Valley&amp;zip=&amp;street=&amp;within=20&amp;day=0&amp;lang=&amp;orderby=distance">Same with NA</a> - some online meetings because most are not meeting in person.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></strong></p>
<p><strong>Mental Health Focus</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>NAMI </strong>(National Alliance on Mental Illness) Lehigh Valley - NAMI offers general support through phone in addition to support groups on zoom for general, family and peer support.<br>Online:&nbsp; <a href="http://www.nami-lv.org">www.nami-lv.org</a></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Support group info:<br><a href="http://www.nami-lv.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/zoom-support-group-flyer-pdf.pdf">http://www.nami-lv.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/zoom-support-group-flyer-pdf.pdf</a>&nbsp;<br><br></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Open Path Collective </strong>-&nbsp; During these challenging times, we remain dedicated to connecting clients with therapists offering affordable care. Many of our therapists are transitioning to online sessions until this crisis passes (and still, there are some that will work with you, in-person).&nbsp; To search for online therapy, look for the online therapy option in the left sidebar. Then, choose your state. You will then see a list of therapists who are licensed in your state to work online with you.&nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Online: <a href="https://openpathcollective.org/">https://openpathcollective.org/</a><a href="https://openpathcollective.org/"><br><br></a></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Muhlenberg College</strong> - campus resource page:<br><a href="https://www.muhlenberg.edu/offices/counseling/informationforstudents/self-helpforstudents/usefullinks/">https://www.muhlenberg.edu/offices/counseling/informationforstudents/self-helpforstudents/usefullinks/</a><a href="https://www.muhlenberg.edu/offices/counseling/informationforstudents/self-helpforstudents/usefullinks/"><br><br></a></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Psychology Today </strong>- search for local providers and filter by insurance, zip code, etc.<br> Online: <a href="http://www.pyschologytoday.com">www.pyschologytoday.com</a><a href="http://www.pyschologytoday.com"><br><br></a></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Virtual Therapy Tools </strong>- there are many online resources available at this time to find a therapist online.&nbsp; There are also many virtual tools including applications to support mindful action.<br><br></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Mindfulness Apps</strong> -&nbsp; Some of the apps below have a cost, while others are free.<strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;">Mindshift - uses and teaches Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) tools to help alleviate anxiety. Features include: Coping skills, journal, check-in, healthy habits etc. <em>Free</em></p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;">Headspace - Relaxation, meditation and mindfulness tools. Annual fee but offering a free content package during pandemic. Educator/student discount as well. Limited free features, $95/year</p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;">Calm - Relaxation and meditation with main focus on healthy sleep and mindfulness. Limited free version, $70/year</p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;">Happify - Focus on gratitude, empathy and healthy living with tips, activities and games. Extensive free features, $12/month</p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;">Mood Path - Geared towards managing depressive symptoms. Free with In-app purchases</p>
<p style="padding-left: 60px;">Sanvello - Free access during pandemic; self-care courses, track habits, goals, mood. Geared towards managing depressive symptoms.&nbsp;<br><br></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>Insurance</strong> - Most insurance plans include behavioral health as part of the benefits. Be sure to check with your insurance provider that behavioral health is included and if you have an individual/family deductible you need to meet. You may also want to familiarize yourself with the out-of-network policy in case the provider you choose is out-of-network. During the pandemic, some insurance companies have temporarily waived copays for behavioral health visits.&nbsp;</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">To Find a Therapist Through Insurance: Call the number on the back of your insurance card to hear a list of providers in your area. Alternatively, visit your insurance network‚s website to obtain a more comprehensive list. Call or send an email to the provider you‚re looking to work with to check their availability as well as if they are accepting new clients.&nbsp;</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Internet Search: Open search engine and type ‚Mental Health + zip code‚ or ‚Counselor + Zip code + speciality‚ for example: ‚Therapist + 18045 + anxiety‚. Visiting their profiles or websites can give you an idea of what type of therapy they offer and see if they‚d be a good fit.
