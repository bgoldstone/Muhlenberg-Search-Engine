<h1 dir="ltr"><span><a id="General Support Information"></a>General Support Information</span></h1>
<p><span><strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Preference is for Panopto to be used when creating new videos as the migration from Kaltura occurs. Our documentation on this page continues to grow based on user interests and needs. Please contact us at </span><a href="mailto:itms@muhlenberg.edu"><span style="font-weight: 400;">itms@muhlenberg.edu</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> should you have any needs, questions, and/or requests for additional documentation.</span></strong></span></p>
<p dir="ltr">Have video from a campus event or wish to share academic video content with the public? Please contact Media Services for assistance, such as associating videos on&nbsp;Video@Muhlenberg&nbsp;at the category level.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>The ITMS team can also assist faculty and students with creating videos within Panopto and within Canvas.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>For academic videos which are not your own productions and for related copyright questions, </span><a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/lendingservices/coursereserves" target="_blank"><span>please contact Trexler Library staff to inquire about Trexler Reserves.&nbsp;</span></a></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>Know that Panopto has a</span><a href="http://trust.panopto.com/" target="_blank"><span> </span><span>status update webpage</span><span> </span></a><span>related to any known/reported issues or maintenance.&nbsp;<strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">They also have an on-demand </span><a href="https://howtovideos.hosted.panopto.com/Panopto/Pages/Folders/DepartmentHome.aspx?folderID=4b9de7ae-0080-4158-8496-a9ba01692c2e"><span style="font-weight: 400;">video repository</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> about many of the most commonly-asked about items and about the various Panopto features.</span></strong></span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>In addition to the ITMS and OIT team support, you also have access to Panopto support resources 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Muhlenberg faculty and staff may contact Panopto Support at </span><span><a href="mailto:support@panopto.com">support@panopto.com</a>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1_1bIqSlsM8Fvjgjx5lUvUqXr64RvIFBYyY_b0wWB0hc/edit?usp=sharing" target="_blank">use this document for hours of support, types of support, and additional means of contacting</a> Panopto Support.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">We are also</span></strong><a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/10ix1Exit-GEngNyR_ZFxakVIS0DnhjMU307Wd5zYDhQ/edit?usp=sharing" target="_blank"> developing a frequently asked questions document here</a>,<strong> <span style="font-weight: 400;">including tips on how to use Panopto shortcuts like hotkeys on Mac and PC devices.</span></strong></p>

<h1 dir="ltr"><span><a id="I'm new to this"></a>I'm new to this</span></h1>
<p dir="ltr"><span>To learn about the "getting started" first steps into Panopto, please refer to these respective Panopto links for&nbsp;<a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/basic-recording-1" target="_blank">Windows</a> and for&nbsp;<a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Recording-with-Panopto-for-Mac" target="_blank">Mac.</a></span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>To use the desktop client, here are the respective</span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Install-Panopto-for-Windows" target="_blank"><span> </span><span>Windows</span></a><span> and</span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Install-Panopto-for-Mac" target="_blank"><span> </span><span>Mac</span></a><span> </span><span>installation resources. Also, there are details on the </span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/How-to-Use-the-Panopto-Mobile-App" target="_blank"><span>Panopto mobile app here.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></a></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span><strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">These Panopto resources discuss </span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Panopto-for-Windows-Custom-Primary-Quality-Settings"><span style="font-weight: 400;">optimal screen settings and configurations for devices while using Panopto.</span></a></strong></span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>This </span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Record-a-Video" target="_blank"><span>Panopto resource discusses how to record a video</span></a><span>.</span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Record-a-Video"><span> </span></a><span>Do note that you may use Panopto without a webcam, and that there is ability within the platform to adjust what secondary tracks are used as well.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>You may upload</span><span> </span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Batch-Upload-Video-Files" target="_blank"><span>existing video files into Panopto</span></a><span>.</span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Batch-Upload-Video-Files"><span> </span></a><span>You can </span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Batch-Copy-and-Move-Videos" target="_blank"><span>batch copy and move Panopto videos</span><span>.</span><span>&nbsp;</span></a></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>Automatic captions are completed with each new video. You will need to go </span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/ASR-Generated-Captions" target="_blank"><span>through these steps to activate and edit them</span></a><span>.&nbsp;<strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Do note that Panopto link mentions that there is a delay from the initial activation until the captions show (can be 8 hours for an hour videoâs captions to appear as an example).</span></strong></span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>You may upload</span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Manually-upload-Captions" target="_blank"><span> caption files manually</span></a><span> if you had them edited in Kaltura previously.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span>To </span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/Use-My-Folder" target="_blank"><span>use the folders in Panopto, there is the following documentation.&nbsp;</span></a><span><strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Caution: Once a Panopto folder is created within Canvas, it must remain in its original location. This will avoid any access issues or interruptions.</span></strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">To get Zoom recordings in Panopto, please refer to this </span><a href="https://muhlenbergcollege.hosted.panopto.com/Panopto/Pages/Viewer.aspx?id=9f22eefe-553e-4c59-a0c6-acad00f50c7e"><span style="font-weight: 400;">one minute ITMS video</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and the </span><a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1L0S7LuN8RC32OGkRKTinI8M3LQZKqWF-BmXAw_o5OxA/edit?usp=sharing"><span style="font-weight: 400;">accompanying documentation on how the Zoom to Panopto process works</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></strong></p>
<h1 dir="ltr"><span><a id="It's a new semester"></a>It's a new semester</span></h1>
<p dir="ltr"><span>Refer to the "I'm new to this" section above for the steps related to creating videos.</span></p>
<p dir="ltr"><span><strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">When it comes to moving Panopto videos from one course to another, please refer to this </span><a href="https://support.panopto.com/s/article/merge-and-copy-1">Panopto guide on copying and moving sessions.</a>&nbsp;<span style="font-weight: 400;">Caution: Once a Panopto folder is created within Canvas, it must remain in its original location.
