<h4 style="text-align: center; max-width: 625px; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"><strong>Muhlenberg College 2022 Summer Intern Housing Program will commence on Friday, May 27th, 2022.<br><br>Online registration for Summer 2022 will be available here in early 2022. </strong></h4>
<div class="style3"><hr>
<p class="center" style="text-align: left;"><strong>2022</strong></p>
<p class="center" style="text-align: left;"><strong>Summer Corporate Internship</strong></p>
<p class="center" style="text-align: left;"><strong><strong>Housing available at Robertson, South, The Village</strong> <strong>and 2201 Chew Street</strong></strong></p>
<p class="center" style="text-align: left;"><strong>Muhlenberg College</strong></p>
<p>The housing offered at Robertson, South, The Village and 2201 Chew Street includes luxury, apartment-style living in a picturesque, wooded, park like setting. Each apartment is fully furnished and the kitchen is equipped with a full set of appliances.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The kitchen in Robertson and South contains a refrigerator, microwave and sink. There are&nbsp;two&nbsp;large recreational rooms with full kitchens, a television and a second floor mezzanine area.&nbsp;</p>
<div class="gmail_default"><strong><span>Housing is offered at a rate of $24.00 per day.&nbsp; This entitles you to a private bedroom within an apartment-style suite.&nbsp; The remainder of the suite is shared space.</span></strong></div>
<div class="gmail_default"><strong><span>&nbsp;</span></strong></div>
<div><strong><span>Note:&nbsp; If occupants move-in or move-out on any other day than the 1st or last day of the month, monthly rate will be pro-rated&nbsp;based on the number of days within that month.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></strong></div>
<p align="left"><img src="/media/contentassets/images/about/events/VillageInteriorKitchen-s.jpg" alt="Conference & Event Services" style="     " /></p>
<p align="left"><strong><img src="/media/contentassets/images/about/events/VillageInteriorLivingRoom-s.jpg" alt="Conference & Event Services" style="     " /></strong></p>
<p><strong>Application</strong><strong> Information:</strong></p>
<p>Interns must complete and submit an application. Once the application has been reviewed and accepted by Muhlenberg College, a lease agreement will be forwarded to you with additional payment and move-in instructions.</p>
<p>The lease can be signed at check-in, but applications must be submitted by April 15, 2022. Preference will be given to applications received by <strong>April 1</strong><strong>,</strong><strong> 2022.</strong></p>
<p>Final Application Due Date<strong>:</strong><strong> May 1</strong><strong>,</strong><strong> 2022</strong></p>
<p>Late applications may take up to 2 weeks to process</p>
<p>* Please note: Until further notice, we are not accepting any applications which are less than a 60-night consecutive stay.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Intern&nbsp;</strong><strong>Housing&nbsp;</strong><strong>Information:</strong></p>
<p>All housing for Summer 2021 Interns will be located in one of the following dorms: Robertson, South, The Village or 2201 Chew Street. All dorms are non-smoking.</p>
<p>2201 Chew Street suites can house up to 4 interns (2 doubles). Robertson, South and The Village suites can house up to 4 interns all in separate bedrooms.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Assignment&nbsp;</strong><strong>Information:</strong></p>
<p>If there are fewer than 4 applications from an intern program, we reserve the right to house interns from different programs together. Suites may be co-ed, but only if all interns in the suite approve.</p>
<p>Roommate/Suitemate requests will be honored only if both request each other.</p>
<p>If any applicants feel that they may need specific housing accommodations, please contact JoEllen Rooney at 484-664-3810 (or via email <a href="mailto:joellenrooney@muhlenberg.edu">joellenrooney@muhlenberg.edu</a>) to discuss arrangements or accommodations.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Extended Stay:</strong></p>
<p>Muhlenberg Summer Intern housing is available May 27th&nbsp;â August 7th, 12:00 pm. For those needing housing before or after these dates may we suggest a local extended stay hotel:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.reservationcounter.com/hotels/show/6255ee3/homewood-suites-by-hilton-allentown-bethlehem-center-valley-center-valley-pa" target="_blank">Homewood Suites, Center Valley</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.guestreservations.com/hilton-garden-inn-allentown-west/booking" target="_blank">Hilton Garden Inn, Fogelsville</a></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>2022 Check-In&nbsp;</strong><strong>Information:</strong></p>
<p>Check-in will only be available during scheduled office hours or pre-arranged check-in times. Check-in Available:</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Friday, May 27th from 12pm â 6 pm<br>Saturday, May 28th from 10 am â 6 pm<br>Sunday, May 29th from 10 am â 6 pm<br>Memorial Day:&nbsp;May 30th</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Office&nbsp;</strong><strong>Hours:</strong></p>
<p>The Seegers Union Information Desk Summer Hours are: <strong>Mon - Fri 8:30 am - 4:30 pm</strong></p>
