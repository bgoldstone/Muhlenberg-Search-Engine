  <h3><strong>Racial Justice Organizations</strong></h3>
<p><strong><br>National Organizations</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://secure.actblue.com/donate/ms_blm_homepage_2019"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Black Lives Matter</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">A global organization working to eradicate white supremacy and build local power to intervene in violence inflicted on Black communities.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://advancementproject.org/home/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Advancement Project</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: A </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">multi-racial civil rights organization dedicated to human rights struggles for equality and justice in order to fulfill Americaâs promise of a caring, inclusive and just democracy.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://www.aclu.org/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">American Civil Liberties Union</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Nonprofit dedicated </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">to defend and preserve the individual rights and liberties guaranteed to every person in this country by the Constitution and laws of the United States</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://www.joincampaignzero.org/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Campaign Zero</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">:&nbsp;Organization that utilizes research-based policy solutions to end police brutality in the U.S.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://colorofchange.org/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Color of Change</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">The nationâs largest online racial justice organization</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://www.themarshallproject.org/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Marshall Project</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">A nonpartisan, nonprofit news organization that seeks to create and sustain a sense of national urgency about the U.S. criminal justice system.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://org2.salsalabs.com/o/6857/p/salsa/donation/common/public/?donate_page_KEY=15780&amp;_ga=2.209233111.496632409.1590767838-1184367471.1590767838"><span style="font-weight: 400;">NAACP Legal Defense Fund</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">Americaâs premier legal organization fighting for racial justice.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="http://nationalbailout.org/about/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">National Bail Out</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">A Black-led and Black-centered collective of organizers, lawyers and activists building a community-based movement to support Black Americans and end systems of pretrial detention and mass incarceration.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://www.showingupforracialjustice.org/why-surj.html"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Showing Up for Racial Justice (SURJ</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">): A multi-racial coalition dedicated to undermining white supremacy and help build a racially-just society.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://unicornriot.ninja/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Unicorn Riot</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">:&nbsp;Nonprofit media collective dedicated to exposing the root causes of social, economic, and environmental issues.</span></p>
<p><strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></strong></p>
<p><strong>Minnesota</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="https://www.gofundme.com/f/georgefloyd">George Floyd Memorial Fund</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">:&nbsp;The official GoFundMe to support the Floyd family.&nbsp;<strong>(Goal Reached)</strong></span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://minnesotafreedomfund.org/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Minnesota Freedom Fund</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Community-based fund set up to pay criminal bail and immigration bonds for individuals who have been arrested while protesting police brutality. This has become one of the most prominent bail funds, providing relief to protesters in Minneapolis seeking justice for George Floyd.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://www.blackvisionsmn.org/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Black Visions Collective</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Minnesota-based black, trans, and queer-led organization committed to dismantling systems of oppression and violence.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://www.reclaimtheblock.org/home"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Reclaim the Block</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Coalition that advocates for and invests in community-led safety initiatives in Minneapolis neighborhoods.</span></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://actionnetwork.org/fundraising/public-goods/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Public Goods</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">:&nbsp;Mutual aid fund for the residents of Minneapolis public housing, organized by Defend Glendale &amp; Public Housing Coalition (DGPHC).</span></p>
<p><strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong></strong></p>
<p><strong>Georgia</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><a href="https://actionnetwork.org/fundraising/support-justiceforgeorgefloyd-protesters-in-atlanta"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Atlanta Solidarity Fund</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Action Network fund set up to support the George Floyd protesters with both bail and necessary legal relief.</span></p>
