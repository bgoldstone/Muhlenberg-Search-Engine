      <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Trexler Library is eager to continue to support you and your students as you prepare for spring courses, both online and in-person. The following information highlights procedural updates and information relevant to the current teaching and learning environment.&nbsp; These changes take priority over existing documentation.</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="#reserves">Course Reserves Updated Procedures for Spring 2021</a></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="#consultations">Librarian Consultations</a></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="#purchases">Requesting Library Purchases for Spring 2021</a></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="#contactless">Contactless Checkout for Library Materials</a></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="#ill">Interlibrary Loan Spring 2021</a></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Collection Resources Online</strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Currently available: Over 1.7 million eBooks, eJournals, eVideos, eMusic, articles, databases and more are immediately findable and accessible through</span><a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/"><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Encompass</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and </span><a href="http://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/az.php"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Databases A-Z</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. In Encompass, you can narrow search results to any of the above e-format types, by using the Format filters on the left side of the search results screen (</span><a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/onlineresources/accessingonlineresources/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">examples</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">). <br></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Resources with COVID-19 free temporary access have been removed; one exception - all JSTOR archive journals are freely available until June 30, 2021. <br></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In addition to the above, we call your attention to the following:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Streaming videos: Through Alexander Street, the library provides unlimited subscription access to </span><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://video.alexanderstreet.com/channels"><span style="font-weight: 400;">over 70,000 videos across most of our disciplines</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Content types range widely and include instructional videos, fiction, documentaries, news and event recordings, and more. Through other publishers and platforms streaming access is purchased for a limited term on a title-by-title basis.&nbsp;</span></li>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Submit requests using the </span><a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/facultyresources/suggestapurchaseform/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">purchase request form</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and include the course number.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1AtSpFZ8yHSpmHUZsVQ1M9sn1b_mEcl0IVIgqV_BIdsE/edit?usp=sharing"><span style="font-weight: 400;">See the Streaming Film Services document</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> for additional guidance for streaming requests.</span></li>
</ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Textbooks: As a general policy the library does not purchase textbooks, however we have acquired some as part of ebook package purchases. These are cataloged and findable in </span><a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Encompass</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> along with catalogued Open Access textbooks. This subject guide provides sources for finding </span><a href="http://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/c.php?g=760834&amp;p=5875901"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Open Educational Resources</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Linking to library resources from within Canvas: View this</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> <a href="https://muhlenbergcollege.hosted.panopto.com/Panopto/Pages/Viewer.aspx?id=aa5787d5-1a3b-41f4-b0d1-acea003e397a" target="_blank">short video</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">&nbsp;for guidance. As always, when using online library resources in your course, it is best to link to the document or resource rather than downloading. This assures compliance with license terms and communicates use of these resources to the libraryâs collection development team. For assistance with linking, please email trexlerreserves@muhlenberg.edu or libraryresources@muhlenberg.edu.</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>Course Reserves</strong><a id="reserves"></a></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We will be continuing the COVID-19 best practices adopted for the fall semester by focusing on electronic reserves (physical reserves will be temporarily unavailable for this semester). We will still need our minimum 5-7 day notice requirement; given that we will be working remotely part of the time, this will give us the time needed to explore purchasing digital or streaming options as well as making copies available to you in Canvas. Articles, e-books/e-book chapters, and links to library resources may also be placed on course reserve within Canvas. After identifying a resource, simply fill out our&nbsp;<a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd6oJnnZPAlZYYjUsaPdpfjZNZHHfIz7InH0FR3C6mrecDvGg/viewform" target="_blank">Course Reserves Request Form</a> with the desired links included. </span></p>
<h3><strong>Feature &amp; Drama Film Reserves Practices</strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We request 5 to 7 days advance notice to allow staff time to provide you with streaming access that meets legal requirements. This involves:</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Exploring and purchasing streaming options for feature/dramatic or documentary films (or transcoding up to 25 percent without permission, per the TEACH Act, per our College attorney) or exploring other means of access.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To submit a reserves request, please use the </span><a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd6oJnnZPAlZYYjUsaPdpfjZNZHHfIz7InH0FR3C6mrecDvGg/viewform"><span style="font-weight: 400;">online course reserves request form</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. <br></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">For e-reserves questions, contact Amanda Riegel (trexlerreserves@muhlenberg.edu). <br></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">For copyright questions, contact Kelly Cannon (kellycannon@muhlenberg.edu).</span></p>
<h3><strong>Librarian Consultations<a id="consultations"></a></strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Trexler Libraryâs librarians will continue to be available for consultations about classes, assignments, resources, and student support. They can be reached by email or phone. Contact information can be found on the </span><a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/onlineresources/research/#d.en.426579"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Off-Campus Research Support page</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
<h3><strong>Requesting Library Purchases<a id="purchases"></a></strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Funds are available to purchase new ebooks and streaming films for courses taught this academic year. There will not be separate departmental allocations and requests can be submitted directly to your librarian liaison</span><strong>*</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Submit requests as soon as possible: online resources can take more time to research, acquire, and set up. Purchase requests should include the course number and be emailed directly to your librarian liaison</span><strong>*</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> or submitted through the online </span><a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/facultyresources/suggestapurchaseform/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Suggest a Purchase</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> form. All long-term purchase requests (not for use in Spring) may be submitted to your departmental or interdisciplinary liaison, as appropriate for the request. These requests should be held by the liaison for future submission. <br></span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">More information is available on the </span><a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/facultyresources/collectiondevelopment/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Collection Development page</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">. </span><a href="https://tinyurl.com/y2mnu9fw"><span style="font-weight: 400;">See the Streaming Film Services document</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> for additional guidance for streaming films. <br></span></p>
<p><strong><em>*</em></strong><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">Librarian liaisons: <br></span></em></p>
<p><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">Sciences - </span></em><a href="mailto:rhamelers@muhlenberg.edu"><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">Rachel Hamelers</span></em></a><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">; Social Sciences - </span></em><a href="mailto:jessicadenke@muhlenberg.edu"><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">Jess Denke</span></em></a><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">; Humanities, Business, GCE - </span></em><a href="mailto:kcannon@muhlenberg.edu"><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">Kelly Cannon</span></em></a><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">; Art - </span></em><a href="mailto:tinahertel@muhlenberg.edu"><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">Tina Hertel</span></em></a><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">; Interdisciplinary Programs - </span></em><a href="mailto:pennylochner@muhlenberg.edu"><em><span style="font-weight: 400;">Penny Lochner</span></em></a></p>
<h3><strong>Contactless Checkout of Trexler Libraryâs Physical Materials<a id="contactless"></a></strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">To access Trexler Libraryâs print and audiovisual resources, please review our </span><span style="font-weight: 400;"><a href="/library/onlineresources/accessingprintresources/">Contactless Checkout Service</a></span><span style="font-weight: 400;">. This service maximizes access to physical collections while enhancing safety for patrons and library staff.</span></p>
<h3><strong>Interlibrary Loan<a id="ill"></a></strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Trexler Library is still filling all interlibrary loan requests (books and articles) as normal. As some libraries are still not loaning books, it may take more time to receive book requests or we may be unable to receive a book request.<br><span style="font-weight: 400;">If you have an Interlibrary loan book coming due, please email </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">illd@muhlenberg.edu</span><span style="font-weight: 400;"> to request a renewal or return it using the library Book Drop. If you are not on campus, you may email&nbsp;<a href="mailto:illd@muhlenberg.edu">illd@muhlenberg.edu</a> to request a postage-paid return UPS label.&nbsp; You will need to package the book(s) in a padded envelope or box and drop off the box at your local UPS Store.</span><br></span></p>
